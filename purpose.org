* Discovering Purpose

#+FILETAGS: purpose

** Why it matters

Philipians 3:7-14

- Paul's purpose transcends doing
- Purpose is not something you're supposed to do, but something you're
  supposed to be
  - Doing flows from being
- God's intention is that each person fulfills their highest potential
  in Christ
- Purpose provides:
  - a destination
  - meaning for our past experiences (including achievements,
    mistakes)
  - filter for life decisions and priorities
  - motivation amid setbacks
- Jeremiah 1:5 - God is not random, everything is by divine intention

** What it is not

Luke 12:22-34

- a list of things to have, do or achieve
- what you should be doing, but who you should be becoming
- it is not a decision, but a discovery (2 samuel 5:12)
- it is not a future far-off event, but it is now
- It is not dependant on time, money or connections, they may help or
  hinder
- It is not destiny, we have to actively collaborate with God

** Principles of purpose

Romans 8:27-30

- 2 Timothy 1:9: Divinely inspired
- Isaiah 14:27: Universally applied to all and to each
  - Ephesians 1:9-11
    - God's purpose (the why) informs his will (what, how, when)
    - His purpose is Christ-centered - summing up all things in Him
      - We are predestined according to this purpose
	- Romans 8:28,29: to be conformed to the image of his son
- Ephesians 4:4-7: Individually interpreted
  - Each one has their own unique DNA, fingerprint, sets of
    experiences, skills etc
- Progressively revealed

** Being conformed to the image of his son

Hebrews 1:1-4, John 14:9, John 10:30

- The Son is the exact representation of His being

Jesus revealed that he is:

1) Life :: (1 john 5:11) - salvation, healing, redemption
2) Light :: (John 8:12, 1 John 1:5) - Wisdom, truth, discernment, justice
3) Love (Ahava) :: (John 15:9, 1 John 4:8) - Mercy, Grace, forgiveness

1 John 2:5, 6, 4:12-17

- Our ultimate destination is to be perfected in him.

- Based on the principle of light, life, and love, we turn these into verbs:
  - life -> live
  - light -> learn
  - love -> love
- Our purpose is to live, to learn and to love
  - Live everyday for God's glory
    - physical and spiritual life
  - Learn the lessons we need to learn
    - growth in grace and knowledge of Christ, spiritual wisdom and maturity
  - Love God, be loved by him and share this love with others
    - being perfected in love

- 1 Timothy 2:1-6: The progression:
  - Life as the gate (all to be saved)
  - Light as the path (knowledge of truth)
  - Perfection in love as the destination (conformed to the image of Christ)
    
*** To Live

John 10:9-11

- The enemy comes to steal, kill and destroy
- Jesus came so that we may have abundant life
  - The very life of the eternal God that cannot be stolen, killed or
    destroyed
- Ephesians 2:4-6, John 14:6, 1 John 5:12, Galatians 2:20, Colossians 3:17
  - The Christ gave his life, so that we could receive the
    indestructible life of God
  - Affirmations:
    - We live today for the glory of God being grateful for all things
    - We live today by his power and not our own, Christ will strengthen us

*** To learn

1 John 1:5-7

- The thief operates in darkness - fear, confusion, deception
- Light is the difficult path:
  - The easy path requires nothing and teaches nothing
  - The difficult path requires everything and teaches reality/exposes
    non-reality
  - The difficulties are our internal drama - fear, doubt, worries,
    ego etc
  - We overcome our internal drama by cooperating with God
- John 8:12, John 12:35, Proverbs 4:18,19, Ephesians 5:8, Colossians
  2:6,7, 2 peter 3:18
  - Affirmations
    - We will be humble enough today to learn the lessons we need in
      order to acquire wisdom and maturity
    - Today we will walk in the light as the Christ is in the light,
      learning what the light reveals
    - Today we will grown in knowledge and grace of the Lord Jesus one
      step at a time

*** To Love (Ahava)

Matthew 5:43-48

- Light and Life without love is nothing:
  - Life is the power and strength of love
  - Light is the wisdom and maturity of love

- Difference from new age philosophy
  - It rightly wants love without religion
  - However love without light has no wisdom, and love without life
    has no power
  - Light and Life are found in the Christ

- John 3:16-17, Romans 5:6-10, 1 John 4:16-18
  - Being perfected in love is to be complete, lacking nothing
  - Affirmations:
    - We will today love God, and be loved by him
    - We will today freely receive all that God has given, and freely
      give what we have received
    - We will today remember that when all else fails, love never
      fails
