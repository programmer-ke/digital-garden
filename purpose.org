* Discovering Purpose

#+FILETAGS: purpose

** Why it matters

Philipians 3:7-14

- Paul's purpose transcends doing
- Purpose is not something you're supposed to do, but something you're
  supposed to be
  - Doing flows from being
- God's intention is that each person fulfills their highest potential
  in Christ
- Purpose provides:
  - a destination
  - meaning for our past experiences (including achievements,
    mistakes)
  - filter for life decisions and priorities
  - motivation amid setbacks
- Jeremiah 1:5 - God is not random, everything is by divine intention

** What it is not

Luke 12:22-34

- a list of things to have, do or achieve
- what you should be doing, but who you should be becoming
- it is not a decision, but a discovery (2 samuel 5:12)
- it is not a future far-off event, but it is now
- It is not dependant on time, money or connections, they may help or
  hinder
- It is not destiny, we have to actively collaborate with God

** Principles of purpose

Romans 8:27-30

- 2 Timothy 1:9: Divinely inspired
- Isaiah 14:27: Universally applied to all and to each
  - Ephesians 1:9-11
    - God's purpose (the why) informs his will (what, how, when)
    - His purpose is Christ-centered - summing up all things in Him
      - We are predestined according to this purpose
	- Romans 8:28,29: to be conformed to the image of his son
- Ephesians 4:4-7: Individually interpreted
  - Each one has their own unique DNA, fingerprint, sets of
    experiences, skills etc
- Progressively revealed

** Being conformed to the image of his son


Hebrews 1:1-4, John 14:9, John 10:30

- The Son is the exact representation of His being

Jesus revealed that he is:

1) Life :: (1 john 5:11) - salvation, healing, redemption
2) Light :: (John 8:12, 1 John 1:5) - Wisdom, truth, discernment, justice
3) Love (Ahava) :: (John 15:9, 1 John 4:8) - Mercy, Grace, forgiveness

1 John 2:5, 6, 4:12-17

- Our ultimate destination is to be perfected in him.

- Based on the principle of light, life, and love, we turn these into verbs:
  - life -> live
  - light -> learn
  - love -> love
- Our purpose is to live, to learn and to love
  - Live everyday for God's glory
  - Learn the lessons we need to learn
  - Love God, be loved by him and share this love with others
